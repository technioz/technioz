## Engagement Models

### Project-Based
Billed per project scope. Fixed timeline, fixed deliverables. Best for well-defined projects with clear requirements.

**Typical range**: $10,000 – $100,000+ depending on scope

### Dedicated Team
Monthly retainer for a dedicated squad. Includes PM, developers, QA. Scales up/down monthly.

**Typical range**: $8,000 – $30,000/month depending on team size

### Team Augmentation
Individual engineers embedded in your existing team. No onboarding lag.

**Typical range**: $3,000 – $6,000/month per engineer
