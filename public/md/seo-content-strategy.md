## 1. Overall Strategy Overview

This document provides a complete topic cluster system for Technioz.

- **Pillar Pages**: Broad, authoritative cornerstone content that ranks for head terms.

- **Business Focus**: Every article should eventually drive leads (consultations, quotes, contact).

## 5. Internal Linking Strategy

### Core Rules

1. **Every Cluster Article** must link to its **Pillar Page** (at least 2-3 times naturally).
2. **Pillar Page** must link to all major cluster articles.
3. Link between related clusters when relevant (e.g., AI article → Custom Software article).
4. Link to **service pages** and **contact/consultation** pages from commercial articles.
5. Use descriptive anchor text (avoid "click here").

### Example Linking Flow

- Cost article → Links to: Pillar, "In-House vs Outsourced", "Choose Reliable Partner", Contact page
- AI Agents article → Links to: AI Solutions Pillar, RAG article, LLM Integration article
- Bus Platform Case Study → Links to: Industry Solutions Pillar, Logistics sub-cluster, Contact

- [ ] URL slug contains primary keyword
- [ ] At least one H2 for every 300 words

- [ ] External links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Image alt text with keywords where natural
